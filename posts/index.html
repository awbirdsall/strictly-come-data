Dance Score Predictions

It’s time to predict some scores! Last time, I walked through how I trained three optimized machine learning models to predict Strictly Come Dancing scores, using all available data through Week 3 of the current series, Series 16. All three models performed decently, but the gradient boosting regressor tended to achieve the best accuracy scores in the cross-validation. So, I used that model as the “official” prediction of the week’s scores, though I was interested to see how the other models’ predictions compared.
